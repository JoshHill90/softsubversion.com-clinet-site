    About me

    I'm Carly Brown, a photographer now based in the vibrant city of Nashville, TN, previously starting my business professionally in Portland, OR. My desire to explore visual arts originally began in my hometown in eastern Washington, sparking my deep love for creative expression. While my background initially led me to a career in film production, my post-graduate exploration brought me to a profound appreciation for fashion photography and portraiture. With over four years of experience in this medium, I've developed a particular fondness for film photography in the last four years.

    I found ability to infuse each image with a unique texture and depth sublime, and strive to create an ethereal atmosphere with my images. I truly thrive on exploring new concepts and ideas, welcoming a diverse range of projects! I’m open to shoots consisting of fashion branding, band photos, portraits, couples, live shows, weddings, and other special events. I would be honored to come together and bring your vision to life here in Nashville, TN. I can also travel to you! Let’s discuss and collaborate to create something beautiful together!
